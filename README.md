## The problem it solves

**Daily guitar practice should not start by building a studio session.**

Built for guitarists and bassists who want a focused practice rig on their desktop. Not a DAW. Not a model marketplace. A refined effects matrix, looper, recorder, and tuner — ready when you are.

## Why you should try it

- You want to plug in, tune, build a usable chain, loop a phrase, record a take, and keep playing.
- You do not want hundreds of plugins, DAW routing, and setup friction before every practice session.
- You need a small offline rig for tone, timing, repetition, and quick review.

## What makes it strong

### Plug in and hear the instrument

- Connect through an audio interface, monitor the input, and shape a usable practice tone without opening a DAW session.
- The rack is intentionally focused: tuner, effects matrix, looper, recorder, presets, and meters instead of a huge production environment.
- Local desktop operation keeps practice files, presets, loops, and recordings on the machine where you play.

### The effects matrix covers daily work

- Community includes the core board: noise gate, exciter, 8-band EQ, overdrive, delay, and reverb.
- Preview effects before adding them, drag blocks into the signal chain, reorder the rack, and adjust knob-led parameters without leaving the playing surface.
- Professional and Team unlock the full catalog, including neural amp, pedal capture, cabinet IR, modulation, dynamics, and deeper time-based controls.

### Looper, tuner, and recorder stay in reach

- The chromatic tuner shows note, cents, and frequency so setup stays fast before effects hide pitch problems.
- Record and overdub loops, play against the phrase, clear it, and build the next repetition without switching apps.
- Capture practice takes to WAV so you can hear timing, bends, muting, and tone decisions after the run.

### $19 lifetime for a focused rig

- Community includes tuner, the core effects board, looper, recorder, and presets for the core practice loop.
- Professional is a one-time $19 lifetime license for the full rack, neural amp, cabinet IR workflow, and global BPM-aware time effects.
- Buy it when you want a reliable practice surface, not another subscription, amp-library maze, or full recording project.

## What you can do with it

- **Low-latency input monitoring** — Connect your guitar through an audio interface, choose a small buffer, and keep the monitoring path focused for real-time practice.
- **Drag-in pedalboard matrix** — Preview effects from the catalog, drag them into the signal chain, reorder blocks, bypass without losing edits, and save the rack as a preset.
- **Looper, recorder, and tuner** — Record and overdub loops, capture practice takes to WAV, and tune up with a fast FFT-based chromatic tuner that shows note, cents, and frequency.
