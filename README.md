## The problem it solves

**Daily guitar practice should not start by building a studio session, and it should not cost you a monthly plugin subscription either.**

Built for guitarists and bassists who play daily and don't want a subscription for it. Not a DAW. Not a marketplace charging per amp model. Sixteen studio-grade effects, a looper, recorder, and tuner — the whole rack, free, the moment you plug in.

- You want a neural amp modeler, real cabinet IRs, and a full pedalboard — without paying per model or per month (a real alternative to ToneX, Neural DSP, and Helix Native for daily practice).

## The full rack, unlocked from note one

Sixteen studio-grade effects — noise gate, compressor, boost, pitch shift, overdrive, neural amp, pedal capture, exciter, 8-band EQ, cabinet IR, chorus, phaser, tremolo, delay, and reverb — all free to play, all at once. No effect is locked behind a tier. Chain a neural amp capture into a real cabinet IR and a synced delay on day one, for $0. The only thing Professional unlocks is unlimited saved presets — everything you hear stays free.

### Amp captures and cabinets that actually sound like the room

- **Neural Amp** plays back captured amp heads with their gain staging intact — pick a model, set gain, go. No modeling menus, no latency tax.
- **Cabinet IR** convolves your signal with real impulse responses — the difference between a line signal and a mic'd rig, without a microphone.
- **Pedal Capture** runs snapshots of real drive pedals, so a favorite stomp box lives inside the rack next to the amp.

### Console-grade control, tactile by design

- **Boost** is a long-throw motorized fader with a dB scale, unity notch, and post-fader LED peak meter — not a knob pretending to be one.
- **Exciter** adds harmonic shimmer above the fundamental so single notes cut through a mix without turning up the volume.
- Eight-band **EQ**, **Phaser**, **Tremolo**, and a **Reverb** that can freeze a tail open forever — modulation and space effects with a synced tempo underneath.

### Loop it, record it, tune it, keep it

- Record a phrase, overdub layers on top, and jam over the loop — your own personal backing band, wired into the same rack as your effects.
- Capture practice takes to WAV and hear timing, bends, and tone decisions after the run — a fast FFT tuner keeps setup honest before you start.
- Save the chain as a preset — 3 slots free, unlimited with a one-time $19 Professional license. That's the whole upgrade.

## What you can do with it

- **Low-latency input monitoring** — Connect your guitar through an audio interface, choose a small buffer, and keep the monitoring path focused for real-time practice.
- **Sixteen-effect signal chain** — Drag effects from the catalog into the chain, reorder blocks, bypass without losing edits, and save the rack as a preset — every module unlocked from the first launch.
- **Looper, recorder, and tuner** — Record and overdub loops, capture practice takes to WAV, and tune up with a fast FFT-based chromatic tuner that shows note, cents, and frequency.
